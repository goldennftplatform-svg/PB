Round ledger

Two buckets. Fixed numbers. No fuzzy jackpot math.

Problem we solve: You seed a $10k SOL jackpot and run a meme promo. The meme 3×'s but the displayed prize barely moves. Players get confused. Round ledger separates SOL committed (fixed prize) from meme bonus (fixed token count, USD floats).

Bucket 1 — SOL jackpot (every round)

Admin opens a round with a committed lamport amount. That's what ODD pays from — 50% main, 5% × 8 minors, 10% house reserve.

Live wallet balance can grow from tax drip and LP harvest, but the committed number is what you promised for that draw cycle.

Recipient% of committed SOL

Bucket 2 — Meme callout (rare rounds)

Admin enables a callout → 10% of pre-snapshot SOL buys an announced meme mint once. We record raw token count at buy time.

On ODD payout, meme splits by % of tokens — not USD. If the coin moons, winners get a bonus. SOL math unchanged.

Recipient% of meme stash (tokens)

EVEN rollover

Pepe count even → no SOL payout that round. SOL commitment and meme stash roll forward. Hype builds. Next ODD hits harder.

On the live app

When a round is active, the homepage shows a Round ledger strip: round ID, SOL committed, optional meme stash, ODD/EVEN outcome.

Admin updates via CLI, then export-public for the site:

node scripts/round-ledger.js open --sol 10
node scripts/round-ledger.js record-snapshot --pepe 7 --seed <SEED>
node scripts/round-ledger.js export-public
