Prize

Winners receive SOL from the jackpot wallet. Game tokens are entry keys only — the sauce is “buy these coins, win SOL.”

Tickets (combined USD at snapshot)

Sum value across all registered game mints per wallet. No double-counting master/yin.

Meme callout (rare)

Most rounds: SOL only. On special admin-enabled rounds, the jackpot may buy a announced meme coin (10% of pre-snapshot SOL) before the draw outcome is known.

On ODD payout rounds with a callout, meme tokens split the same way as SOL (60 / 8×4 / 6 / 2). Not every draw — rare bonus spice.

Provably fair

  • VRF / on-chain randomness for winner index
  • Holdings snapshot published before draw
  • All transfers verifiable on Solscan
