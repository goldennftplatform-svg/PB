1 — Get SOL

You need SOL for swaps and tx fees. Use Coinbase, OKX, Gemini, or any on-ramp. Send SOL to your Phantom (or supported) wallet on Solana.

2 — Buy game tokens

Swap SOL → game token on the live app (Jupiter / Pond widget) or pump.fun / trix.market when launched. No separate “ticket purchase” — your holding at snapshot time is your entry.

3 — Spread smart (optional)

Combined USD across master + Pump shell + TRiX bridge counts toward tickets. $500 total split any way = max 4 tickets — you don't need $500 in each coin.

4 — Wait for snapshot

Admin runs a holdings snapshot before each draw. If you still hold enough combined value, you're in automatically. Check “Who's in the draw” on the live app.

5 — Draw & payout

Pepe ball count (1–30): ODD = SOL payout to winners. EVEN = rollover — committed prize rolls, hype builds. Verify on Solscan.

6 — Round ledger (transparency)

Each round locks a fixed SOL commitment. Rare meme bonuses use a fixed token count. See round ledger guide — shown on the live app when active.
